About Me

My name is Hannah Boinowitz. I'm a graduate student of Data Science at the TU Braunschweig. I previously studied Physics with a minor in Computer Science at the same university.
I enjoy coding a lot and would definitely consider it one of my hobbies.
When I was still in high-school I've taught myself some Python fundamentals. Ever since I was hooked by the idea that I can use a computer for my needs and constantly practiced to get better at it.
In the last years, especially during my university studies, I have gained some experience when it comes to coding in scientific contexts. Two internships in the software industry and my working student job in Data Science have helped me to gain additional hands-on software engineering skills in industrial settings.
